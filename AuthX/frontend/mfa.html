Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

Multi-Factor Authentication strengthens account security by requiring multiple verification steps before granting access.

🔐 Knowledge Factor

Username and password known only to the user.

📲 Possession Factor

One-Time Password (OTP) sent to registered device or email.

🧠 Human Verification

Ensures login is performed by a real human, not a bot.

🕒 Session Validation

Validates session time and prevents unauthorized reuse.

🌍 Device & Location Check

Detects unfamiliar devices or unusual login locations.
