Device & Location Check

Device & Location Check is an advanced security layer in Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA). It verifies whether a login attempt originates from a trusted device and an expected geographic location, helping to detect suspicious or unauthorized access.

What Comes Under Device & Location Check

  • Trusted or registered devices
  • IP address and network verification
  • Geographic location detection
  • Browser and device fingerprinting

Authentication Flow

  1. User completes previous MFA steps.
  2. System identifies the device and browser details.
  3. Login location is compared with known locations.
  4. If risk is detected, additional verification is required.

Security Benefits

  • Prevents logins from unknown devices
  • Detects unusual or high-risk locations
  • Reduces account takeover attempts
  • Enhances overall MFA protection
Note: Device & Location Check works alongside Knowledge, Possession, Human Verification, and Session Validation factors to detect unusual login behavior and prevent unauthorized access.
