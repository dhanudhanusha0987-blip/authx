Threat Detection

Threat Detection identifies abnormal and suspicious login behavior to prevent unauthorized access and security breaches.

🚨 Suspicious Login Attempts

Detects repeated failures, brute-force, and credential-stuffing attacks.

🌍 Unusual Login Locations

Flags logins from unexpected countries or geographic regions.

💻 Unknown or Risky Devices

Identifies access from new, untrusted, or compromised devices.

📊 Behavioral Anomalies

Monitors unusual login timing, frequency, and access patterns.
